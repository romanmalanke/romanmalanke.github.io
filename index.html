Interests

Growing Wealth Inequality

The wealth of billionaires increased from 3% to 14% of global GDP in the last few decades. In France, for example, 30 years ago 500 richest individuals owned 6% of GDP and today they own 42%. I support the work of Gabriel Zucman, Thomas Piketty, Gary Stevenson and others who campaign for reforming the taxation systems to prevent ultra-rich from being able to pay less effective income tax than all other social classes.

Misinformation & Manipulation

Technological advances that were meant to democratize people’s access to information are exploited by dictatorships to manipulate and keep power. The overwhelming majority of media outlets are owned by ultra-rich people who push their self-serving agendas rather than objective reality. The average person’s attention span and critical thinking are decreasing. I support independent media, true journalism, and I dream of finding a scalable way to reduce the general public’s susceptibility to informational manipulations.

Russia’s War Against Ukraine

No shades of grey here. Trying to subjugate a neighboring country by force, using genocidal methods to eliminate the Ukrainian identity and distort real history. I support funds that help Ukraine and the whole civilized world to win this war. Join me in donating to Come Back Alive and Sternenko Foundation.
